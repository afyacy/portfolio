ABOUT

As a kid, my attention was on drawing and solving maths. Seemingly in my own world of doodles and drawings. I will appreciate things based on how they look and nothing else... sounds childish though.

Growing up, I've worked with a printing press where we design and print things like a business card, brochure, poster, banner and sticker. Not forgetting the experiences with a team of application developers in building softwares...

When I started college I originally had the idea of going into networking. After building my first website and getting instant gratification of praise from others, I was hooked. The internet was my outlet. A new way to show and share my passions. Now I work as a graphic designer and a web developer.

I do the work because I love it. I like the tech industry. I like the people I meet. I embrace the nerd and I love to make the web a better place. Hopefully we cross paths in the real world someday and we can make it a better place together ):
